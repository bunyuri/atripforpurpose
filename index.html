Our Safaris

Explore our range of safari experiences.

Mountain Gorilla Safari-4 Days

Experience the best of Rwanda with our shortest safari, featuring an unforgettable encounter with mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park. Guests can also opt to visit the golden monkeys or trek to Dian Fossey’s grave for added adventure.

Starting from 1000$/person

Primates of Rwanda Safari-7 Days

This safari offers exclusive primate trekking experiences in Rwanda, including the mountain gorillas, chimpanzees, golden monkeys, and colobus monkeys, with the chance to spot even more species. The adventure takes you to both Volcanoes National Park in the north and Nyungwe National Park in the south.

Starting from 1000$/person

Rwanda Discoverer-7 Days

This safari offers the chance to visit both gorillas and chimpanzees, as well as explore the savannah of Akagera National Park in eastern Rwanda. Here, you'll see a diverse array of animals and birds.

Starting from 1000$/person

Best of Rwanda-10 Days

This safari combines visits to the gorillas and chimpanzees, explores the savannah of Akagera National Park with its diverse wildlife and birdlife, and concludes with a relaxing trip to the stunning white-sand beaches of Lake Kivu in northwestern Rwanda, offering the perfect end to a "Best of Rwanda" tour.

Starting from 1000$/person
